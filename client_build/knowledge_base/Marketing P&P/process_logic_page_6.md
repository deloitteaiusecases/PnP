### Analysis of the Flowchart

1. **Process Name**: 
   - Merchandising Activity

2. **Roles** (Swimlanes):
   - Trade Marketing Manager
   - Merchandising Specialist

3. **Steps in Markdown Table:**

   | Step # | Role                    | Action                                                                                       | Next Step/Logic                        |
   |--------|-------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------|
   | 1      | Trade Marketing Manager | Develop planogram of each Key accounts and share it with Head of Marketing for approval. (M) | Step 2                                 |
   | 2      | Trade Marketing Manager | Share approved Planogram and any promotions detail with 3rd party Merchandising agency. (M)  | Step 3                                 |
   | 3      | Merchandising Specialist| Responsible for planogram and promotion execution. Communicate issues to Key Account Manager and B2C Sales Director. (M) | End                                    |
