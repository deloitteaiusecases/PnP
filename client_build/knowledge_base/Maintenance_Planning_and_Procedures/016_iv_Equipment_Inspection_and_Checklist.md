## iv	Equipment Inspection and Checklist

Introduction and Background of the Section
Regular equipment inspections are a critical component of proactive and preventive maintenance at Arabian Mills. Inspections ensure early detection of abnormalities, compliance with safety and hygiene standards, and preservation of operational reliability. In both wheat milling and animal feed plants—where dust, mechanical wear, and high-speed components are constant factors—structured inspections reduce unplanned downtime, quality deviations, and safety incidents.
To ensure standardization and traceability, all inspections must be conducted using approved checklists, documented through the SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) module, and linked to corrective or follow-up actions as required.
Procedure for Inspection and Checklist

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Define Inspection Requirements**<br>• Identify equipment types requiring periodic inspections based on OEM guidance, failure history, and criticality. | Maintenance Manager | Annual / As Needed |
| 2 | **Develop Standard Checklists**<br>• Create inspection checklists for each asset class or inspection type (e.g., mechanical, lubrication, safety). | Maintenance Manager | Initial + Reviewed Annually |
| 3 | **Integrate Checklists into SAP**<br>• Embed inspection checklists in SAP task lists or work order operation texts to standardize execution. | SAP PM Administrator | One-Time / Updates as Needed |
| 4 | **Schedule Inspection Activities**<br>• Generate work orders based on inspection schedules and assign to technicians. | Maintenance Planner | Monthly Planning Cycle |
| 5 | **Conduct Equipment Inspections**<br>• Execute inspections using printed or digital checklists. Identify non-conformities or potential failures. | Technician | As Scheduled |
| 6 | **Record Findings in SAP**<br>• Enter inspection results, condition codes, observations, and follow-up need directly into the work order feedback. | Technician | After Each Inspection |
| 7 | **Create Follow-up Work Orders**<br>• If issues are identified, raise corrective notifications and initiate work orders through SAP. | Maintenance Supervisor | As Needed |
| 8 | **Review Inspection Compliance and Findings**<br>• Review compliance reports, missed inspections, and emerging issues for proactive intervention. | Maintenance Supervisor | Monthly |
| 9 | **Update Checklists and Scope**<br>• Revise checklists based on field experience, failures, or asset upgrades. | Maintenance Manager | Annual / As Needed |

Checklist Types
Checklists should be customized by equipment type and inspection frequency:

| Checklist Type | Application |
| --- | --- |
| Daily Visual Checklist | Conveyors, belts, pneumatic lines, motors |
| Weekly Condition Checklist | Sifters, elevators, dust collectors, compressors |
| Monthly Functional Checklist | Roller mills, feeders, pelletizers, filters |
| Safety/Hygiene Checklist | Explosion vents, bearing seals, lubrication systems |

All checklist templates must be controlled through document management and versioning.
Roles and Responsibilities – Equipment Inspection

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Technician | • - Conduct equipment inspections using approved checklists.<br>• - Record findings, condition codes, and feedback in SAP.<br>• - Raise notifications for abnormalities or potential failures. |
| Maintenance Supervisor | • - Review inspection feedback and ensure accuracy of entries.<br>• - Escalate critical findings and create corrective work orders.<br>• - Monitor completion of scheduled inspections. |
| Maintenance Planner | • - Schedule inspections in SAP and assign technicians and checklist templates.<br>• - Coordinate workload with production to ensure inspection windows are met. |
| Maintenance Manager | • - Define inspection content, scope, and intervals based on failure history, OEM standards, and criticality.<br>• - Review inspection findings to update preventive strategy. |
| Maintenance Director | • - Ensure policy compliance and use of standardized checklists.<br>• - Monitor inspection KPIs and lead reliability-focused reviews with planners and managers. |
| SAP PM Administrator | • - Maintain checklist templates, ensure version control, and link checklists to SAP task lists.<br>• - Support troubleshooting of system issues affecting inspection workflows. |
