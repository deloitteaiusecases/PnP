## Computerised Maintenance Management System

Introduction and Background of the Section
The CMMS, powered by SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) at Arabian Mills, is the central platform that integrates all maintenance activities—planning, scheduling, execution, spare parts management, performance tracking, and reporting.
Its effectiveness depends not just on system functionality, but also on the quality of data entered, disciplined usage by all stakeholders, regular audits, and timely updates to master data. A well-maintained CMMS ensures visibility, accountability, decision-making based on real data, and alignment with best practices such as ISO 55000, SMRP, and RCM (Reliability-Centered Maintenance).
The purpose of this section is to define the strategic activities required to maintain and continuously improve the SAP PM system, ensuring it remains accurate, reliable, and supportive of the maintenance department’s goals.
Procedure for CMMS Maintenance and Governance

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Enter and Maintain Equipment Master Data Integrity**<br>• Ensure all assets in SAP have complete and correct specifications, functional locations, and classifications. | SAP PM Administrator | Ongoing / Monthly |
| 2 | **Enter, Review and Update Task Lists and Plans**<br>• Ensure PM task lists reflect actual field practices and are updated based on lessons learned or equipment changes. | Maintenance Planner | Quarterly |
| 3 | **Standardize Notification and Work Order Fields**<br>• Enforce standard naming, fault codes, and feedback formats to ensure data consistency. | SAP PM Administrator | Monthly Review |
| 4 | **Monitor System Usage Discipline**<br>• Ensure technicians, planners, and supervisors enter timely and accurate data. Highlight and address misuse or gaps. | Maintenance Manager | Monthly Review |
| 5 | **Run MRP and Validate Stock Linkages**<br>• Ensure that MRP runs are generating relevant spare part demands from maintenance plans and work orders. | SAP MM Administrator | Weekly |
| 6 | **Clean Up Obsolete or Duplicate Entries**<br>• Remove or archive inactive assets, duplicate spares, or closed work orders beyond retention window. | SAP PM Administrator | Quarterly |
| 7 | **Manage User Access and Roles**<br>• Define, assign, and periodically review SAP access levels for maintenance users. | SAP PM Administrator | Semi-Annually |
| 8 | **Train Users on CMMS Functions**<br>• Conduct training for new users and refresher sessions for existing users on best practices for notifications and closures. | SAP Admin | As Needed |
| 9 | **Evaluate CMMS Effectiveness**<br>• Use KPIs such as work order cycle time, system usage rates, data quality index, and PM compliance to assess SAP value. | Maintenance Director | Quarterly |

Roles and Responsibilities – CMMS Management

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| SAP PM Administrator | Maintain master data, enforce naming standards, audit system usage, clean duplicates, manage access rights |
| SAP MM Administrator | Support MRP execution and ensure correct linkage of maintenance spares to task lists and BOMs |
| Maintenance Planner | Keep task lists and maintenance plans current and relevant |
| Maintenance Director | Drive system discipline, approve training programs, evaluate KPIs, and escalate system-related issues |
| Technicians and Supervisors | Enter accurate, complete, and timely maintenance feedback into SAP |
