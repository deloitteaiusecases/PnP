## Record Keeping and Documentations

Introduction and Background of the Section
Record keeping is not only a regulatory requirement but a practical necessity for ensuring operational consistency, safety, and traceability. At Arabian Mills, effective documentation underpins maintenance planning, performance management, continuous improvement, and environmental compliance.
This sub-section standardizes the way documents and records are created, approved, stored, and retrieved by the Maintenance & Engineering Department. It applies to both internally generated records and external documentation provided by OEMs and service vendors.
Types of documents covered under this section include:
 Work Orders and PM Completion Reports
 Equipment History Logs
 Inspection and Safety Checklists
 Calibration Certificates and Schedules
 Spare Parts Inventory Logs
 RCA Reports and Corrective Action Logs
 Energy and Waste Logs
 Vendor Evaluations and Service Records
 Training and Competency Records
 OEM Technical Manuals, Datasheets, Installation Reports, and Compliance Certificates
All records, whether paper-based or digital, must be accurate, complete, version-controlled, and accessible for audit, analysis, and reference.
Procedure – Record Keeping and Documentation

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Identify Mandatory Records**<br>• Define and approve all documentation categories required for compliance and performance. | Maintenance Director / QA / EHS | Annual Review |
| 2 | **Use Standardized Templates and Formats**<br>• Ensure records such as inspections, permits, and evaluations follow approved formats. | Planner / Safety Officer / Maintenance Manager | Ongoing |
| 3 | **Document All Maintenance Activities**<br>• Record job details, time spent, spares used, and feedback in the assigned forms or SAP. | Technician / Supervisor | Daily / Per Job |
| 4 | **Review and Approve Completed Records**<br>• Verify completeness, accuracy, and signatures before storing or closing out. | Maintenance Supervisor | Daily / Weekly |
| 5 | **Store Records Securely**<br>• Use SAP for system-based records and organized physical files for manual documentation. | Planner / Storekeeper | Ongoing |
| 6 | **Maintain a Master Document Register**<br>• Track the location, version, and retention date of all key documents. | Maintenance Planner | Monthly Review |
| 7 | **Backup and Archive**<br>• Digitally back up critical records and archive completed records as per retention policy. | SAP Administrator / Document Controller | Quarterly |
| 8 | **Ensure Accessibility for Audits**<br>• Make records available for internal and external audits upon request. | Maintenance Manager / Planner | As Needed |
| 9 | **Conduct Record Audits**<br>• Randomly verify the accuracy and completeness of maintenance documentation. | QA | Quarterly |
| 10 | **Maintain External Documentation**<br>• Collect, categorize, and store all OEM manuals, technical datasheets, service records, calibration certificates, and installation reports. | Maintenance Manager / Planner | Upon Receipt / Ongoing |

Roles and Responsibilities – Record Keeping and Documentation

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Technician | - Complete all work order documentation accurately and promptly. |
| Maintenance Supervisor | - Review documentation for accuracy and completeness before closing work orders or submitting reports. |
| Planner | • - Maintain document templates, master registers, and ensure filing consistency.<br>• - Track external documents and file location. |
| Maintenance Manager | • - Maintain records related to RCA, energy efficiency, vendor evaluation, and corrective actions.<br>• - File OEM and service documentation. |
| Storekeeper | - Document spare issuance, slow/obsolete parts, and consumption records. |
| Safety Officer | - Ensure safety permits, PPE checklists, and incident forms are completed and filed. |
| SAP Administrator | - Support system documentation, backups, and user access control. |
| Maintenance Director | - Oversee compliance with documentation standards and audit readiness. |
