## Key Contact Information

Introduction and Background of the Section
In any industrial facility, particularly one with continuous operations like Arabian Mills, access to accurate and up-to-date contact information for key internal and external personnel is essential for effective communication, rapid response to breakdowns, and coordination of services.
Maintenance & Engineering personnel frequently need to contact:
 Internal stakeholders (e.g., production supervisors, safety officers, warehouse staff)
 External service providers (e.g., calibration contractors, OEMs, utility vendors)
 Emergency responders (e.g., fire safety, medical assistance)
 Compliance authorities (e.g., environmental regulators, inspection bodies)
This section standardizes how such contact information is maintained, updated, and accessed—whether for routine coordination, urgent escalation, or documentation purposes.
Procedure – Key Contact Information Management

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Develop a Standard Contact List Template**<br>• Create a unified format to list name, designation, role, phone, email, and availability. | Planner / Admin | One-time |
| 2 | **Maintain Internal Contact List**<br>• Include all relevant maintenance, engineering, production, safety, and store personnel. | Planner / Admin | Monthly Update |
| 3 | **Maintain External Contact List**<br>• Include vendors, OEMs, utility companies, calibration labs, and emergency services. | Maintenance Manager / Admin | Monthly / As Needed |
| 4 | **Ensure Accessibility of Contact List**<br>• Keep updated copies in shared drives, maintenance office, control room, and CMMS if applicable. | Maintenance Manager | Ongoing |
| 5 | **Flag Critical Contacts**<br>• Clearly identify emergency or critical contacts (e.g., after-hours support, safety hotline). | Planner / Safety Officer | Quarterly Review |
| 6 | **Verify and Update Contacts**<br>• Confirm contact validity with each stakeholder/vendor regularly. | Planner / Admin | Biannually |

| --- | --- |
