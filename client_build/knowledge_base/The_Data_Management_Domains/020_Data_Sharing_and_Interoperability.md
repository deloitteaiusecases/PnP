## Data Sharing and Interoperability

Data Sharing and Interoperability involves gathering data from diverse sources and comprises of integration strategies that promote smooth internal and external communication between various IT components. Data sharing processes that enable a structured and uniform exchange of data between entities are also covered by data sharing and interoperability.
