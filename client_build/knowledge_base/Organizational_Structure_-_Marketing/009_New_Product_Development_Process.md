## New Product Development Process

##### Policy Statement
 Launching new products categories required significant resources, collaborated efforts and market research.
 All new product ideas should support the company’s strategic goals, market positioning, and brand promise.
 A business case must be developed for each proposed product, outlining objectives, target market, competitive positioning, and financial projections.
 A cross-functional NPD Committee shall be established with representation from Marketing, Sales, QA, Production, Supply Chain, Legal and Finance.)
 All NPD projects shall be managed under the supervision of this committee to ensure alignment and timely execution.
 Ideas may be sourced internally (staff, operations feedback) or externally (customer feedback, market trends, competitor benchmarking).
 All ideas shall be screened considering feasibility, profitability, scalability, and strategic fit.
 R&D shall lead formulation trials using lab-scale or pilot plant equipment.
 Finance shall review and approve costing, pricing strategy, and margin targets.
 Any new product shall have XX gross margin to be considered for launch.
 For launching any new product category, which is beyond core capabilities and expertise of Arabian Mills, third party manufacturer shall be used till significant volumes are achieved which can justify CAPEX expenditures.
 Marketing and QA shall jointly approve all packaging artwork and claims.
 A go-to-market plan shall be prepared, including product positioning, pricing, promotion strategy, and sales training.
 Moreover, while studying market, it will be equally important to understand consumer sentiments, therefore, Usage and attitudinal studies will be crucial to understand the category dynamics and consumer behaviour towards it. A 3 to 5 years innovation pipeline should be locked to work cohesively.
 Launch approval must be obtained from the CEO keeping in loop Head of Sales and as per the DOA.
 Sales performance, customer feedback, quality complaints, and cost metrics shall be monitored post-launch.
 A formal review shall be conducted 6 to 12 months after launch to determine success and decide on scaling, modification, or discontinuation.
 Lessons learned from each NPD project shall be documented to improve future projects.
 All internal Sample testing will be led by Quality team and R&D teams. At least two samples should be developed and tested against benchmark competitor product in blind testing.
