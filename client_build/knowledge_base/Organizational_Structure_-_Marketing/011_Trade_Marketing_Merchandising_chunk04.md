### Steps

| Step # | Role                  | Action | Decision/Next Step |
|--------|-----------------------|--------|--------------------|
| Start  | Key Accounts Manager  | Start | Proceeds to Step 1. |
| 1      | Key Accounts Manager  | Receives draft Agreement/ Promotion Proposal from the Modern Trade/Key Accounts customer (M). | Proceeds to Step 2. |
| 2      | B2C Director          | Shares the draft agreement with recommendations to Trade Marketing Manager for review. (M) | Proceeds to Step 3. |
| 3      | Trade Marketing Manager | Review the draft agreements/proposal in meeting to discuss both departments’ recommendations. (M) | Proceeds to Step 4. |
| 4      | Trade Marketing Manager | Negotiate draft agreement/Proposal with the customer. Revised draft agreement/proposal is submitted to Head of Marketing and Head of Sales for approval. (M) | Proceeds to Step 5. |
| 5      | Trade Marketing Manager | In case of Agreement, submit draft agreement to Legal and then to CFO for approval. In case of proposal, submit proposal to CFO for approval. (M) | If the draft is an Agreement: submit to Legal and then CFO for approval, then proceed to Step 6. If the draft is a Proposal: submit to CFO for approval, then proceed to Step 6. |
| 6      | Trade Marketing Manager | Share approved draft agreement to customer for further process. (M) | Proceeds to Step 7. |
| 7      | Customer              | Submits official signed agreement along with required documentations for official agreement execution. (M) | Proceeds to End. |
| End    | Trade Marketing Manager (location of end symbol) | End | Process ends. |

##### Procedure
The following procedures shall be followed for trade marketing merchandising activities:

| S No. | Procedure description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Planogram Development :**<br>• The Trade Marketing Manager develop planogram of each Key accounts and share it with Head of Marketing for approval . | **Preparer / Performed By : Trade Marketing Manager**<br>• Reviewer: B2C Director and Marketing Manager<br>• Approver:<br>• Head of Marketing | Frequency: As required |
| 2 | **Sharing with Merchandising Agency :**<br>• Trade M arketing Manager will share approved Planogram and any promotion s detail with 3 rd party Merchandising agency along with Merchandising specialist for execution. | **Preparer / Performed By :**<br>• Trade Marketing Manager .<br>• Executer : Agency and Merchandising specialist. | Frequency: As required |
| 3 | **Coordination and Reporting**<br>• Merchandising Specialist is responsible for planogram and promotion execution , on ground checking and coordination with agency . Any issue relevant to stocks or sales should be communicated to Key Account Manager and B2C Sales director via email by Trade Marketing Manager or vis versa . Moreover, weekly report from Merchandising agency be shared via email with Sales team. A monthly meeting should be convened between agency, Marketing, and sales teams to discuss issues and review agency performance. | **Preparer / Performed By : Merchandising Specialist**<br>• Reviewer:<br>• Trade Marketing Manager, Head of Marketing , B2C Sales director & Key Accounts Manager | Frequency: Monthly |
