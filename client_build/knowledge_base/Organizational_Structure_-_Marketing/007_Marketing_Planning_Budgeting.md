## Marketing Planning & Budgeting

##### Policy Statement
 The Marketing Department shall consider annual marketing planning and budgeting as a key activity. To create effective plans, the department shall clearly understand the business strategic goals.
 The Marketing Department shall develop marketing plans based on business goals and in close collaboration with the Sales and Finance departments; plans shall not be developed in isolation.
 Before the start of the new fiscal year, the CEO shall submit draft strategic goals and plans to the Arabian Mills Board for review and approval by September of the current fiscal year. Once the Board approves the plan, the CEO shall submit it to the Marketing Department to prepare the annual marketing plan and budget.
 The Planning and Budgeting process, including approval from the CEO, shall be completed by November to allow ample time for preparation for the next fiscal year.
##### Procedure
The following procedures shall be followed to prepare the Annual Marketing Plan and Budget:

| S No. | Procedure description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Receive Strategic Goals from Board/ Management :**<br>• The Head of Marketing receives the Board approved strategic goals and plan s (one, three and five years) of the organization from the Company Secretary via email by September of the current fiscal year . | Preparer / Performed By : Top Management/Board | Frequency: Yearly |
| 2 | **Reference working :**<br>• The Head of Marketing receives reference working for the annual marketing budget for next fiscal year from CFO or FP&A Manager via email by beginning of the second half of the current fiscal year .<br>• Note: the reference budget working is either based on Fixed amount against total volume of the business or Percentage of Sales revenue. | Preparer / Performed By : FP&A Manager | Frequency: Yearly |
| 3 | **Preparation of Annual Marketing Plans and budgets :**<br>• Upon receiving the business goals and reference budget working, Marketing Manager prepares Annual Marketing Plans and Budgets using MS Excel by October . The Marketing Plans and budget clearly delineate the following elements along with respective budget :<br>• Corporate Brand building activities.<br>• Brand Equity Building activities of existing brands. (strategic investment )<br>• Promotional activities (tactical spending including trade marketing )<br>• New Products/Brand launc hes<br>• Note:<br>• New Products/Brands Marketing budgets will be developed and shared separately from existing brands . Trade marketing spending will be developed separately and be incorporate in wholistic Marketing budget. | **Preparer / Performed By : Marketing Manager and Trade Marketing Manager**<br>• Reviewer: Head of Marketing | Frequency: Annually |
| 4 | **Review with Sales team**<br>• The Head of Marketing and Head of Sales discuss es Marketing Plan and annual sales forecast to ensure alignment between marketing initiatives and sales targets.<br>• The review is conducted through a formal meeting, and any inconsistencies are resolved coll aboratively.<br>• After alignment, the same is submi tted to CFO by early October via email for review and feedback . | Reviewers : Head of Marketing and Head of Sales | Frequency: Annually |
| 5 | **Review and Approval**<br>• The CFO reviews the Plan and Budget and may request clarifications or revisions. Upon CFO approval, the final plan is submitted to the CEO for final sign off no later than early November.<br>• Once CEO approves the plan and budget, the same is consolidated as part of the annual budget pack and submitted to Board for approval.<br>• If the CFO or CEO does not approve the submission, the Head of Marketing revises the plan based on the feedback received and procedures 3, 4 and 5 are repeated until final approval is obtained. | **Preparer / Performed By : Head of Marketing**<br>• Reviewer: CFO<br>• Approver: CEO | Frequency: Annually |
