The following procedures shall be followed for trade marketing activities except merchandising:

| S No. | Procedure description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Customer Share it s Proposal:**<br>• The Key Accounts Manager receive s draft Agreement/ Promotion Proposal from the Modern Trade/Key Accounts customer and submit s it to B2C Director for Review within one day of receiving it. | **Preparer / Performed By : Key Accounts Customer**<br>• Reviewer: B2C Director. | Frequency: As required |
| 2 | **Review of Proposal by Marketing :**<br>• B2C Director share s the draft agreement/proposal with his recommendations via email to Trade Marketing Manager for review . This process shall be com pleted within 2 days of receivin g from Key Account Manager. | **Preparer / Performed By :**<br>• B2C Director .<br>• Reviewer: Trade Marketing Manager<br>• Approver: Head of Marketing | Frequency: As required |
| 3 | **Joint Discussions**<br>• Trade Marketing Manager along with Head of Marketing , Head of Sales, B2 C Sales Director , Key Account Manager and Marketing Manager review the draft agreements / proposal in meeting to discuss both departments’ recommendations.<br>• After internal consensus is reached between Sales and Marketing, the draft agreement/proposal is shared with the CFO for review of financial aspects .<br>• If the CFO suggest s changes, the draft agreement is revised and resubmit ted for approval.<br>• Marketing team develops 2 to 3 counter proposals or agreements for negotiating purpose.<br>• The entire process is completed within 7 days of receiving the recommendation from B2C Sales Director. | **Preparer / Performed By : Trade Marketing Manager**<br>• Reviewer: Head of Marketing, Marketing Manager , Head of Sales, B2C Sales director , Key Account Manager<br>• Approver: CFO | Frequency: As required |
| 4 | **Negotiation with customer**<br>• Trade Marketing Manager a nd Key Account Manager or B2C Director negotiate draft agreement/Proposal with the customer . After alignment with customer , revised draft agreement /proposal is submitted to Head of Marketing and Head of Sales for approval. | **Preparer / Performed By : Trade Marketing Manager , Key Account Manager**<br>• Reviewer: Customer , Director B2C Sales<br>• Approver:<br>• Head of Marketing & Head of Sales | Frequency: As required |
| 5 | **Review of revised draft agreement /Proposal by Finance & Legal**<br>• In case of Agreement , Trade Marketing Manager submit draft agreement to Legal and then to CFO for approval.<br>• In case of proposal, Trade marketing Manager submit proposal to CFO for approval. | **Preparer / Performed By : Trade Marketing Manager**<br>• Approver : CFO | Frequency: As required |
| 6 | **Sharing of Approved draft agreement**<br>• Trade Marketing Manager share approved draft agreement to customer for further process. | Preparer / Performed By: Trade Marketing Manager |  |
| 7 | **Official Agreement /Execution**<br>• After approval from CFO, in case of agreement , customer submit official signed agreement along with required documentations for official agreement execution . This document should be officially signed by B2C Sale Director or Trade Marketing Manager and witnessed by CFO and Legal.<br>• In case of promotions /activities proposals , Trade Marketing Manager give email approval to customer and keep in loop Head of Marketing and B2C Sales director and Key Account Manager. | • Prepare r/Performed by : Customer.<br>• Approver: B2C Sales Director , Trade Marketing Manager, CFO and Legal | Frequency: As required |

### Steps

| Step # | Role                  | Action | Decision/Next Step |
|--------|-----------------------|--------|--------------------|
| Start  | Key Accounts Manager  | Start | Proceeds to Step 1. |
| 1      | Key Accounts Manager  | Receives draft Agreement/ Promotion Proposal from the Modern Trade/Key Accounts customer (M). | Proceeds to Step 2. |
| 2      | B2C Director          | Shares the draft agreement with recommendations to Trade Marketing Manager for review. (M) | Proceeds to Step 3. |
| 3      | Trade Marketing Manager | Review the draft agreements/proposal in meeting to discuss both departments’ recommendations. (M) | Proceeds to Step 4. |
| 4      | Trade Marketing Manager | Negotiate draft agreement/Proposal with the customer. Revised draft agreement/proposal is submitted to Head of Marketing and Head of Sales for approval. (M) | Proceeds to Step 5. |
| 5      | Trade Marketing Manager | In case of Agreement, submit draft agreement to Legal and then to CFO for approval. In case of proposal, submit proposal to CFO for approval. (M) | If the draft is an Agreement: submit to Legal and then CFO for approval, then proceed to Step 6. If the draft is a Proposal: submit to CFO for approval, then proceed to Step 6. |
| 6      | Trade Marketing Manager | Share approved draft agreement to customer for further process. (M) | Proceeds to Step 7. |
| 7      | Customer              | Submits official signed agreement along with required documentations for official agreement execution. (M) | Proceeds to End. |
| End    | Trade Marketing Manager (location of end symbol) | End | Process ends. |
