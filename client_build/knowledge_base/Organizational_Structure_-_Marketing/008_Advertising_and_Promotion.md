## Advertising and Promotion

##### Policy Statement
 All advertising and promotional activities will be derived from approved Annual Marketing Plans and budgets.
 All advertising and promotional activities must align with the company’s brand guidelines, core values, and strategic marketing objectives.
 A formal campaign plan should be prepared for all major advertising or promotional activities, including objective, target audience, media channels, and estimated budget.
 All campaign plans should undergo internal review and approval by the Head of Marketing before execution.
 Budget estimates for each campaign should be reviewed and approved by the CFO and CEO prior to execution.
 Only approved creatives (ads, videos, POS materials) may be published and will adhere to regulatory standards and brand compliance.
 All content should be truthful, respectful, and free of misleading or unsubstantiated claims, especially regarding product benefits.
 Campaigns should be monitored throughout the campaign duration.
 Post-campaign analysis, including key performance indicators (KPIs), ROI, and lessons learned, documented, and submitted within 30 days of campaign closure.
 All advertising and promotion expenses should be tracked against approved budgets and reviewed for cost efficiency.
 Campaign documents (plans, approvals, creatives, reports) should be archived for a minimum of two years for audit and compliance purposes.
 External vendors and agencies involved in campaigns shall sign NDAs and follow company brand, confidentiality, and quality protocols. Refer SCM policies and procedures for vendor onboarding and purchase order issuance.
 All major or seasonal campaigns, either Brand building or Promotional activities should be communicated to all relevant departments 3 months in advance. Following types of promotional activities and campaigns will be usually undertaken at Arabian Mills:
  o Awareness Building activities.
  o Major Seasonal Promotional activities.
  o Consumer Promotions
 Any variations from approved annual plans, budgets, timings, or ad hoc promotional activities, including trade marketing, must take approval from CFO and CEO and be communicated to all relevant department via email.
 On 1st week of every quarter a Management committee Group meeting (MCM), comprising of all Departments Heads and CEO, shall be convened to Preview previous quarter activities and share upcoming quarter plans of all departments, especially Sales, Marketing and Supply Chain, for organization wide collaboration and alignment.
