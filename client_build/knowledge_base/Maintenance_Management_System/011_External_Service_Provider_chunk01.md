## External Service Provider

Introduction and Background of the Section
External Service Providers (ESPs) play a key role in supporting the maintenance department at Arabian Mills by delivering specialized services that cannot be performed in-house due to technical complexity, regulatory requirements, or equipment constraints. These services include but are not limited to: instrument calibration, certification inspections, specialized mechanical repairs, and non-destructive testing (NDT).
Arabian Mills uses a structured and transparent process to engage, monitor, and evaluate external contractors. All ESP engagements are integrated with SAP for requisitioning, tracking, performance documentation, and invoice reconciliation. This ensures service quality, timely delivery, and cost control.
This system is aligned with ISO 9001 (Quality Management Systems), ISO 17025 (Calibration Services Competency), and internal safety and reliability protocols. The objective is to ensure that external interventions complement internal maintenance programs without disrupting plant operations or safety.
Policy
 Only prequalified and approved external service providers will be engaged for maintenance-related activities.
 All engagements must be based on a formal contract or approved work order, with a clear Scope of Work (SOW) documented and maintained in SAP.
 All engagements must be formalized through SAP purchase requisitions (PR) and purchase orders (PO), linked to maintenance notifications or work orders.
 Safety, confidentiality, and regulatory compliance requirements must be explicitly communicated and enforced.
 Each completed job shall be followed by a performance assessment based on adherence to timelines, quality of work, and documentation standards.
 Invoice processing will follow a structured review, approval, and recording workflow. If budget is not available, the work shall not proceed without express written approval from the Maintenance Director and Finance Department
External Service Providers Procedure

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Identify Need for External Services**<br>• Log need for specialized services (e.g., calibration, NDT, overhaul). | Maintenance Manager | As Required |
| 2 | **Raise SAP Notification**<br>• Create SAP notification tagged as requiring external services. | Supervisor | As Required |
| 3 | **Define Scope of Work (SOW)**<br>• Draft technical specifications, safety requirements, and expected deliverables. | Maintenance Manager | As Required |
| 4 | **Attach SOW to SAP Work Order**<br>• Upload the final scope into SAP for reference in PR and PO generation. | SAP PM Administrator | As Required |
| 5 | **Verify Existing Contract**<br>• Check if the service provider is operating under an existing contract. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 6 | **Select Vendor – Prequalified**<br>• Select an ESP from the approved vendor list based on type of service and past performance. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 7 | **Select Vendor – Not Prequalified**<br>• Initiate qualification process: collect documents, assess technical/commercial capacity, and onboard in SAP. | Procurement Department | As Needed |
| 8 | **Clarify Material Provision Responsibility**<br>• Determine whether the ESP will bring their own consumables/tools, and document in SOW. | Maintenance Manager | Per Job |
| 9 | **Raise Purchase Requisition in SAP**<br>• Create PR referencing SAP work order and scope. | Maintenance Supervisor | As Required |
| 10 | **Review and Approve PR**<br>• Verify PR for accuracy, scope, and budget availability. | Maintenance Manager | As Required |
| 11 | **Check Budget Availability**<br>• Block PR processing if no budget is available. Seek finance clearance or defer work. | Finance Department | As Required |
| 12 | **Convert PR to PO and Issue to Vendor**<br>• Generate and send Purchase Order to vendor. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 13 | **Service Execution**<br>• Vendor delivers service as per contract and scope. | External Service Provider | As Required |
| 14 | **Supervise Service Delivery**<br>• Ensure site coordination and compliance with safety and quality protocols. | Maintenance Supervisor | During Execution |
| 15 | **Collect Certificates/Reports**<br>• Receive calibration reports, inspection certificates, and completion evidence. | Maintenance Manager | Per Job Completion |
| 16 | **Attach Documents to Work Order**<br>• Upload all service documentation in SAP for traceability. | SAP PM Administrator | Per Job Completion |
| 17 | **Evaluate Vendor Performance**<br>• Assess work based on timeliness, quality, communication, and safety adherence using a standardized form. | Maintenance Manager | Per Job Completion |
| 18 | **Receive Invoice**<br>• Collect invoice and verify it against PO and actual work completed. | Procurement Department | Per Job Completion |
| 19 | **Review and Approve Invoice**<br>• Cross-check invoice with performance, documentation, and contract terms. | Maintenance Manager | Per Job Completion |
| 20 | **Record and Process Payment**<br>• Enter approved invoice into SAP and process for payment. | Finance Department | Per Job Completion |

Induction of a New External Service Provider (ESP)
Objective
To ensure that all new external service providers engaged for maintenance work meet Arabian Mills' standards for technical competence, safety compliance, reliability, and commercial viability before being added to the approved vendor list. This procedure ensures a transparent, auditable, and risk-controlled approach to onboarding new maintenance service providers.
Procedure for Induction of a New External Service Provider

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Initiate Vendor Search**<br>• Identify need for a new service provider where no prequalified vendor exists. Define service scope and vendor type required. | Maintenance Manager | As Required |
| 2 | **Gather Vendor Leads**<br>• Search industry sources, directories, past references, and OEM recommendations for potential providers. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 3 | **Request for Company Profile and Credentials**<br>• Request documentation from potential vendors including licenses, client references, certifications (e.g., ISO). | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 4 | **Technical Capability Assessment**<br>• Review the vendor’s qualifications, technical resources, and similar project experience. | Maintenance Manager | As Required |
| 5 | **Commercial Evaluation**<br>• Assess vendor’s financial health, pricing structure, payment terms, and delivery capabilities. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 6 | **Site Visit**<br>• Conduct a visit to vendor’s facility to verify workshop capabilities, safety standards, and quality systems. | Maintenance Manager | As Needed |
| 7 | **Internal Review and Recommendation**<br>• Compile evaluation findings and prepare a summary recommendation for internal approval. | Procurement Department | As Required |
| 8 | **Approval for Onboarding**<br>• Review and approve the selected vendor based on evaluation and departmental alignment. | Maintenance Director | As Required |
| 9 | **Create Vendor Master Record in SAP**<br>• Enter all vendor details including tax ID, contact information, service types, and compliance status. | SAP MM Administrator | Once per Vendor |
| 10 | **Share Safety and Quality Policies**<br>• Provide vendor with plant-specific safety, quality, and documentation requirements. | Safety Officer | Once per Vendor |
| 11 | **Add to Approved Vendor List**<br>• Include vendor in the approved list for applicable maintenance services and notify relevant teams. | Procurement Department | Once per Vendor |

Roles and Responsibilities – Vendor Induction

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Maintenance Manager | Define technical needs, review vendor capabilities, recommend shortlisting, participate in site visits if required |
| Procurement Department | Collect vendor data, evaluate commercial terms, coordinate assessments, compile recommendations, and onboard in SAP |

| Safety Officer | Provide plant safety policies and validate the vendor’s safety readiness |
| Maintenance Director | Approve vendor induction based on technical and commercial evaluations |

Vendor Performance Evaluation for Maintenance Service Providers
Objective
To establish a structured and objective method to assess the performance of external service providers engaged for maintenance-related activities, ensuring service quality, reliability, compliance, and continuous improvement.
Procedure for Vendor Evaluation

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Determine Evaluation Scope**<br>• Identify which vendors require evaluation (e.g., based on frequency, value, criticality of service). | Procurement Department | Quarterly / Annually |
| 2 | **Collect Performance Data**<br>• Gather inputs from SAP (work order completion, delays, and returns) and maintenance team feedback. | SAP PM Administrator | Monthly / Ongoing |
| 3 | **Prepare Evaluation Form**<br>• Use standardized scoring sheet covering quality, timeliness, documentation, communication, safety compliance, and pricing. | Procurement Department | Per Evaluation Cycle |
| 4 | **Assess Quality of Service**<br>• Evaluate accuracy of work, adherence to specifications, and post-service issues. | Maintenance Supervisor | Per Job Completion |
| 5 | **Assess Timeliness and Responsiveness**<br>• Check adherence to agreed service timelines and response times. | Maintenance Planner | Per Job Completion |
| 6 | **Assess Documentation and Compliance**<br>• Review delivery of service certificates, reports, and alignment with HSE requirements. | Maintenance Manager | Per Job Completion |
| 7 | **Assess Commercial and Administrative Conduct**<br>• Review billing accuracy, communication, and adherence to contract terms. | Procurement Department | Per Evaluation Cycle |
| 8 | **Score and Categorize Vendor**<br>• Assign scores and classify vendors (Preferred / Acceptable / Watchlist / Disqualified). | Procurement Department | Quarterly / Annually |
| 9 | **Share Feedback with Vendor**<br>• Communicate performance results, areas for improvement, and corrective expectations. | Procurement Department | Quarterly |
| 10 | **Update Vendor Rating in SAP**<br>• Record evaluation results in SAP vendor master for future reference. | SAP MM Administrator | After Each Evaluation |
| 11 | **Take Corrective Action (if needed)**<br>• Escalate performance issues to management and determine if vendor should be suspended or removed. | Maintenance Director / Procurement Head | As Needed |

Vendor Evaluation Criteria (Typical Weights)

| Criteria | Weight (%) |
| --- | --- |
| Quality of Work | 30% |
| Timeliness & Responsiveness | 20% |
| Safety & Compliance | 20% |
| Documentation & Reporting | 10% |
| Pricing & Commercial Terms | 10% |
| Communication & Attitude | 10% |

Vendors scoring below 70% are flagged for review; below 50% are recommended for deactivation.
