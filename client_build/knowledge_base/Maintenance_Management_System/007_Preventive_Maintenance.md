## Preventive Maintenance

Introduction and Background of the Section
Preventive Maintenance (PM) ensures the proactive servicing of equipment to prevent unexpected failures and maintain operational continuity. At Arabian Mills, PM activities are scheduled weekly and cover all critical milling and packaging equipment. These activities are driven by a structured monthly plan, executed without a full plant shutdown, and supported by SAP Plant Maintenance (SAP PM).
The PM approach is aligned with ISO 14224, Total Productive Maintenance (TPM), and The Society for Maintenance & Reliability Professionals (SMRP) best practices. It includes inspections, adjustments, lubrication, and early detection of anomalies. The system is supported by an in-house planning process and avoids complete shutdowns by staggering tasks through weekdays.
Policy
 Preventive maintenance shall be carried out for all critical and semi-critical assets at frequencies determined by equipment manufacturer guidelines, operational data, and maintenance history, with clearly assigned days for specific areas.
 Preventive maintenance shall be prioritized based on equipment criticality and must not be deferred without formal approval.
 Any deviation from the approved preventive maintenance schedule must be presented in the monthly S&OP meeting and authorized by the S&OP Core Team. The reason for the deviation must be documented in the SAP PM module.
 All PM activities shall be recorded and tracked in the SAP Plant Maintenance system.
 PM shall be executed in operating conditions unless safety requires isolation.
 Technicians shall document findings, including deviations and potential failures, in SAP for follow-up action.
 KPIs such as PM compliance, overdue PMs, and equipment downtime trends shall be monitored monthly and reviewed quarterly.
Detailed Procedure Breakdown

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Define Preventive Maintenance Master Plans**<br>• Develop PM strategies, task lists, frequencies, and parts required for each asset class in SAP. | Maintenance Manager / Planner | One-time / Annual Review |
| 2 | **Generate Maintenance Forecast in SAP**<br>• Run SAP-based forecast of due preventive tasks for the upcoming month. | Planner | Monthly |
| 3 | **Review and Propose PM Schedule**<br>• Review resource availability and production constraints; propose the detailed PM schedule for the upcoming month. | Maintenance Manager | Monthly |
| 4 | **Present PM Schedule in S&OP Meeting**<br>• Present the proposed PM schedule in the monthly S&OP meeting for alignment and approval. | Maintenance Director | Monthly |
| 5 | **Obtain S&OP Core Team Approval**<br>• Ensure the schedule is formally approved; any changes or deferrals must be authorized and recorded in SAP. | S&OP Core Team / Planner | Monthly |
| 6 | **Convert Planned Tasks to Work Orders**<br>• Release work orders for all approved tasks through the SAP PM module. | Planner / Supervisor | Weekly / Ongoing |
| 7 | **Execute Preventive Tasks**<br>• Carry out the maintenance work as per work order details, task lists, and checklists. | Technician / Supervisor | As Scheduled |
| 8 | **Capture Feedback and Close Work Orders**<br>• Enter findings, time confirmations, spares used, and any anomalies into SAP. | Technician / Planner | Upon Completion |
| 9 | **Monitor PM Compliance**<br>• Track completion % of PM tasks, identify deferrals, and analyse root causes. | Maintenance Manager / Planner | Monthly |
| 10 | **Report PM KPIs in S&OP Review**<br>• Report key metrics (PM compliance, backlog, deferrals) in monthly management reviews. | Maintenance Director | Monthly |

Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Maintenance Manager | • - Develop preventive maintenance master plans and task lists in SAP.<br>• - Support analysis of deferrals and PM effectiveness.<br>• - Review proposed PM schedule for feasibility, resource availability, and plant constraints.<br>• - Monitor monthly compliance and backlogs |
| Planner | • - Run monthly maintenance forecasts in SAP.<br>• - Convert approved tasks into work orders.<br>• - Record justifications for deviations.<br>• - Track execution of PM schedule.<br>• - Generate PM performance reports and KPI dashboards for review |
| Maintenance Director | • - Present proposed PM schedule in the monthly S&OP meeting.<br>• - Lead discussion on PM plan alignment with plant operations. |
| S&OP Core Team | • - Review and approve the monthly PM schedule.<br>• - Authorize any deviations or deferrals and ensure they are documented in SAP. |
| Maintenance Supervisor | • - Execute approved PM tasks through work order management.<br>• - Ensure timely field completion of assigned PM activities. |
| Technician | • - Perform preventive tasks as per work order instructions.<br>• - Record feedback, time, and material use in SAP. |
