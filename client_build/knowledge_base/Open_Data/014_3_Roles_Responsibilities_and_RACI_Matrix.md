## .3. Roles, Responsibilities and RACI Matrix

The following roles and responsibilities are applicable to this policy:
 Data Management and Governance Leadership Team: The executive body of  data management & governance is responsible for signing off on any changes, exemption, and exceptions to this policy.
 Data Governance Council: The strategic body of  data management & governance authorizes Open Data information exercises, defines priorities and critical data, and is responsible for approving sharing decisions.
 Data Governance officer: An experienced business domain representative responsible for managing all data management & governance initiatives and changes. The data governance officer overlooks and manages the Open Data requests and monitor the data sharing approaches.
 Stewardship Team: The stewardship team is responsible for attending training and awareness sessions, implement Open Data request process steps, document all Open data requests, respond to queries from Data Owner and Open Data Officer, prepare and share request forms, maintain Open Data register, raise any issue related to Open Data requests, identify and document metadata for open data sets and support on monitoring KPIs.
 Head of : Head of  responsible for managing all data management & governance initiatives and changes. The Head of  overlooks and manages the delivery of open data to the requestor and maintains plans, approve or reject publishing of open data datasets, maintain timelines, budgets and ensuring that progress is made.
 Chief Risk Officer: CRO is accountable for Authorizing Open Data Plan and roadmap, Update Open Data policy, Monitor KPIs and is responsible for Approve any exceptions or changes in Open Data Policy
 Data Owner: The Data Owner is responsible for providing domain-specific executive-level support in open data operations and exercises, communicating the reports of the exercises across the business domain, support implementation of Open Data process, manage and monitor activities of stewardship team, resolve Open Data related issues raised by stewardship team.
 Data Privacy Officer: The Data Privacy Officer is responsible for supporting Open Data activities. Their deep knowledge of the ’s data sharing capabilities allows them to validate the requests, thus ensuring that data is consistent are in line with the required specifications as per the request. Responsible for carrying activities like identify public datasets, publish and maintain open datasets, support on updating Open Data policy, getting feedback for published Open Data and maintaining Open Datasets which are already published.
 Data User: Any individual interacting with the data without having direct control over it. The data users are responsible for raising any issues that surface while interacting with the data.
 Data Specialist: Data Specialist is consulted for create an Open Data Plan including roadmap and assignment of required resources and budget, authorizing Open Data Plan and roadmap, identify and document metadata for Open Datasets.
