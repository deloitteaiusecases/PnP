## Energy Efficiency

Introduction and Background of the Section
Energy is one of the largest variable costs in flour milling and feed production. As part of Arabian Mills’ commitment to operational excellence and environmental responsibility, improving energy efficiency is a strategic priority.
The Maintenance & Engineering Department, under the Maintenance Director, is directly responsible for enabling and driving factory-wide energy efficiency through:
 Ensuring high performance and availability of energy-intensive equipment
 Monitoring plant energy consumption trends and benchmarking performance
 Identifying and correcting energy losses (mechanical, thermal, electrical)
 Supporting project feasibility for energy-efficient upgrades
 Reducing the energy cost per ton of product, a key KPI at Arabian Mills
This section defines the procedures and roles by which the Maintenance & Engineering Department shall contribute to improving energy performance, in close coordination with production, safety, and finance functions.
Procedure – Maintenance & Engineering Role in Energy Efficiency

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Establish Energy Monitoring Points**<br>• Install and maintain sub-meters on major energy consumers (compressors, HVAC, mills). | Engineering Team | One-time / As Upgraded |
| 2 | **Track Energy Cost per Ton of Output**<br>• Collect energy data and correlate with production volumes to calculate monthly KPI. | **Maintenance Manager +**<br>• With maintenance coordin ator | Monthly |
| 3 | **Maintain Mechanical and Electrical Efficiency**<br>• Ensure rotating equipment is aligned, cleaned, and lubricated to reduce load. | Maintenance Technicians / Supervisor | Ongoing / PM-based |
| 4 | **Detect and Repair Air and Steam Leaks**<br>• Conduct regular inspections using visual, auditory, or ultrasonic methods. | Maintenance Technicians / Supervisor | Monthly / Ad hoc |
| 5 | **Replace or Clean Filters Timely**<br>• Ensure airflow systems (compressors, HVAC, dust collectors) are running at low resistance. | Supervisor | As per PM Schedule |
| 6 | **Calibrate Sensors and Controllers**<br>• Ensure correct readings and control responses for temperature, pressure, and flow. | **Maintenance Vendor +**<br>• Automation Team | As Needed |
| 7 | **Support Energy-Efficient Upgrades**<br>• Provide technical input and execution support for VFDs, efficient motors, insulation upgrades, and power factor correction systems. | **Engineering Team +**<br>• Automation Team | As Required |
| 8 | **Analyze Variations in Energy KPI**<br>• Investigate spikes or drops in energy cost per ton; link with equipment condition, production load, or weather. | Maintenance Manager | Monthly |
| 9 | **Train Staff on Energy-Conscious Practices**<br>• Promote awareness on idling, optimal usage, and shutdown procedures. | EHS Officer | Biannually |
| 10 | **Participate in Energy Audits**<br>• Collaborate with utilities and consultants to identify savings across systems and operations. | Maintenance Manager | Annual / As Needed |
