## Backup and Recovery Management

1. Backup Procedures: Develop, approve, and implement backup and restore procedures considering business process requirements.
2. Regular Backup: Take and test backup copies of information, software, and system images regularly in accordance with agreed backup and restore procedures.
3. Disaster Recovery: Provide adequate backup facilities to ensure all essential information and software can be recovered following a disaster or media failure.
4. Remote Storage: Store backups in a remote location at a sufficient distance to escape damage from a disaster at the main site.
5. Protection: Provide appropriate physical and environmental protection for backup information.
6. Testing: Regularly test backup media to ensure reliability for emergency use.
7. Retention Period: Determine the retention period for critical/essential business information.
8. Restoration Testing: Perform periodic restoration testing.
