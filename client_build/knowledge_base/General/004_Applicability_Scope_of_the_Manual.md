## Applicability / Scope of the Manual

This Feed Mill Policy and Procedure Manual applies to all personnel, departments, and operations involved in the manufacture of animal feed at the Arabian Mills facility adjacent to the flour mill. It governs the full operational lifecycle from raw material intake to finished feed dispatch with emphasis on quality, safety, traceability, and regulatory compliance.
It is applicable to:
 Feed mill staff across production, QA/QC, maintenance, logistics, and sanitation functions.
 Cross-functional teams including Procurement, EHS, Supply Chain, and IT (for SAP integration)
 Contractors and third-party service providers involved in feed mill operations.
 Shared utilities or personnel between the flour and feed mills, especially where by-products are used.
The manual covers:
 Raw material receipt, inspection, storage, and inventory management
 Macro and micro dosing, batching, mixing, pelleting, and cooling
 Use of flour mill by-products in feed formulations
 Packaging, labelling, storage, and dispatch of finished feed
 SAP-based traceability, inventory control, and transaction management
 Equipment cleaning, maintenance, and calibration
 In-process quality checks and laboratory testing
 Compliance with GMP+, ISO 22000, HACCP, and local regulations
 Documentation, training, audits, and continuous improvement initiatives
This manual serves as the official reference for all feed mill operations. Any deviations must be recorded, justified, and approved by authorized personnel.
