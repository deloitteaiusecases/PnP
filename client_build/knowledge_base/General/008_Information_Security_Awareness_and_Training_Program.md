## Information Security Awareness and Training Program

1. Education: Provide appropriate awareness education and training to all employees and contractors, with regular updates on policies and procedures.
2. Awareness Program: Establish, approve, and implement a cybersecurity awareness program for staff, customers, consultants, outsourced and temporary staff, technical and non-technical staff.
3. Periodic Conduct: Conduct awareness programs periodically through multiple channels to strengthen cybersecurity awareness and build a positive culture.
4. Content: Cover the latest cyber threats and protection methods in the awareness program.
5. Customized Training: Provide essential and customized training for personnel working directly on cybersecurity tasks.
6. Periodic Review: Review the implementation of the cybersecurity awareness program periodically.
