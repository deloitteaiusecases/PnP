## Email Protection

1. Requirements: Define, document, approve, and implement cybersecurity requirements for protecting email services.
2. Technology Implementation: Implement technology to protect the email system from external malicious attacks.
3. Privileged Access: Control privileged access to the email domain and system.
4. Secure Login: Ensure webmail logins provide secure login and encryption.
5. Email Filtering: Implement advanced and up-to-date email protection techniques for analysing and filtering email messages.
6. Multi-Factor Authentication: Consider multi-factor authentication for remote and webmail access to email service based on associated risks and business requirements.
7. Archiving and Backup: Implement email archiving and backup.
8. Domain Validation: Implement validation of Arabian Mills' email service domains.
