## Accounting principles and policies
## A.Accounting Concept

Arabian Mills financial reporting framework establishes various fundamental accounting concepts on which a set of financial statements shall be based. Following paragraphs provide more information on the accounting concepts:
 Fundamental characteristics of financial information: The financial information presented in the financial statements of the Company must be relevant and faithfully represent what it purports to represent.
o Relevance: The financial information presented by Arabian Mills is relevant for financial decision-making processes. For financial information to be relevant, it must possess:
   Confirmatory value: provides feedback about previous evaluations.
   Predictive value: used as an input to processes employed by users to predict future outcomes
o True and fair view: The financial statements must give a true and fair view of the state of affairs of Arabian Mills at the end of the financial year and / or financial reporting period as required and of the results of the year or period. To present a true and fair view, the financial statements must be complete, neutral, and free from error.
 Enhancing characteristics of financial information: The usefulness of financial information is enhanced if it is comparable, verifiable, timely and understandable.
o Comparability: Arabian Mills financial statements is comparable, with consistent accounting standards and policies applied throughout each accounting period, enabling users to draw insightful conclusions about the trends and performance of Arabian Mills over time.
o Verifiability: The characteristic of verifiability provides assurance that the information faithfully represents what it purports to be representing.
o Timeliness: The financial information of Arabian Mills must be presented within a timescale that is suitable to the users for their decision-making process.
o Understandability: The classification, characterization, and presentation of the financial information of Arabian Mills is such that it is understandable to those who might want to review and use it.
