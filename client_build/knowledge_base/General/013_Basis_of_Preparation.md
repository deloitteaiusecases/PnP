## Basis of Preparation

The Company prepares financial statements in accordance with International Financial Reporting Standards (“IFRS”), that are endorsed in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (“KSA”), and other standards and pronouncements endorsed by the Saudi Organization for Chartered and Professional Accountants (“SOCPA”) (collectively referred to as “IFRS as endorsed in KSA”). These financial statements must be presented in the functional and presentation currency of the Company which is Saudi Riyals (“SAR”) and rounded to the nearest Saudi Riyals (except when otherwise indicated).
