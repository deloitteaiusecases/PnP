## Asset Management

7. Requirements: Define, document, approve, and implement cybersecurity requirements for managing information and technology assets.
8. Inventory: Identify assets associated with information and information processing facilities and maintain an inventory of these assets.
9. Asset Types: Include hardware, software, documents, services, and people in the asset inventory.
10. Ownership: Define owners and custodians for individual assets in the inventory.
11. Lifecycle Management: Ensure asset owners are responsible for the proper management of assets over their lifecycle.
12. Accuracy: Maintain an accurate and up-to-date asset inventory at all times.
13. Impact Analysis: Conduct business impact analysis for all identified assets.
14. Classification: Classify, label, and handle information and technology assets according to contractual, legal, and regulatory requirements.
15. Acceptable Use: Identify, document, and implement rules for the acceptable use of information and assets.
16. Return of Assets: Ensure all employees and external party users return all Arabian Mills assets upon termination of their employment, contract, or agreement.
17. Periodic Review: Review the cybersecurity requirements for managing information and technology assets periodically.
