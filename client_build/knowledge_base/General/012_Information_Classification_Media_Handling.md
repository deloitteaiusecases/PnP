## Information Classification & Media Handling

1. Requirements: Define, document, and approve information security requirements for protecting and handling data and information according to relevant laws and regulations.
2. Implementation: Implement the defined information security requirements for protecting and handling data and information.
3. Classification: Classify information based on legal and contractual requirements, value, criticality, and sensitivity to Arabian Mills.
4. Accountability: Ensure owners of information assets are accountable for asset classification.
5. Protective Controls: Implement protective controls for information based on business needs for sharing or restricting information and legal requirements.
6. Labelling Procedures: Develop, approve, and implement procedures for information labelling according to the information classification scheme.
7. Awareness: Ensure employees and contractors are aware of labelling procedures.
8. Handling Procedures: Develop, approve, and implement procedures for handling assets according to the information classification scheme.
9. Removable Media: Develop, approve, and implement procedures for managing removable media according to the classification scheme.
10. Disposal: Securely dispose of media when no longer required.
11. Transportation: Protect media containing information against unauthorized access, misuse, or corruption during transportation.
12. Periodic Review: Review the cybersecurity requirements for protecting and handling data and information periodically.
13. Verification: Verify that any sensitive data and licensed software have been removed or securely overwritten before disposal or re-use of storage media.
