## Information Security Organization

1. Roles and Responsibilities: Information security roles and responsibilities must be defined, including risk management, asset protection, project management, and segregation of duties.
2. Annual Review: Roles and responsibilities must be reviewed annually or whenever necessary due to changes in the threat landscape, technology, or business strategy.
3. Authorities: Procedures must specify when and by whom authorities (e.g., law enforcement, regulatory bodies) must be contacted.
4. Contacts: Contacts with other authorities, utilities, emergency services, and health and safety must be defined and maintained.
5. Special Interest Groups: Contacts with special interest groups, security forums, and professional associations must be maintained. Membership for security forms and non-profit organizations must be considered.
6. Information Sharing: Information sharing agreements must be established to improve cooperation and coordination for security issues, identifying requirements for the protection of confidential information.
