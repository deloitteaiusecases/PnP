## Information Security in Human Resource

1. Personnel Requirements: Define, document, and approve personnel cybersecurity requirements (before employment, during employment, and after termination/separation).
2. Communication: Communicate information security roles and responsibilities to candidates during the pre-employment process.
3. Screening: Conduct screening checks on all candidates for employment per relevant laws, regulations, and ethics.
4. Contractors: Define and implement screening for onsite and outsourced contractors and consultants.
5. Competence: Ensure candidates have the necessary competence and trustworthiness for specific information security roles.
6. Agreements: Include information security responsibilities in contractual agreements with employees and contractors.
7. Training: Provide information security awareness training to new joiners and during employment.
8. Application: Require all employees and contractors to apply information security in accordance with established policies and procedures.
9. Disciplinary Process: Establish a formal disciplinary process for information security breaches.
10. Post-Termination: Define and enforce information security responsibilities that remain valid after termination or change of employment.
11. Access Review: Review and remove access to information and technology assets immediately upon termination/separation.
12. Periodic Review: Review employee and contractor cybersecurity requirements periodically.
