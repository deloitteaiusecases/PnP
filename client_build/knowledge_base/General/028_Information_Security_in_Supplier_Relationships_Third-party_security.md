## Information Security in Supplier Relationships (Third-party security)

1. Supplier Agreements: Agree on information security requirements for mitigating risks associated with suppliers' access to Arabian Mills assets with the supplier and document these requirements.
2. Contract Requirements: Identify, document, and approve cybersecurity requirements for contracts and agreements with third parties.
3. Establishment: Establish and agree on relevant information security requirements with each supplier that may access, process, store, communicate, or provide IT infrastructure components for Arabian Mills information.
4. Supply Chain: Include requirements to address information security risks associated with information and communications technology services and product supply chain in agreements with suppliers.
5. Monitoring: Regularly monitor, review, and audit supplier service delivery.
6. Service Changes: Manage changes to the provision of services by suppliers, considering the criticality of business information, systems, and processes involved, and reassess risks.
7. Data Removal: Develop and implement non-disclosure clauses and secure removal of Arabian Mills data by third parties upon the end of service.
8. Communication Procedures: Develop third-party communication procedures for cybersecurity incidents.
9. Compliance Requirements: Include requirements for third parties to comply with related Arabian Mills policies and procedures, laws, and regulations in contract agreements.
10. Managed Services: Ensure cybersecurity managed services for monitoring and operations are completely present inside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
11. Periodic Review: Review cybersecurity requirements for contracts and agreements with third parties periodically.
