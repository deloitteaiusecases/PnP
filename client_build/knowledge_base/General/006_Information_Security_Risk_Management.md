## Information Security Risk Management

1. Risk Management Methodology: Establish a risk management methodology based on ISO27005 Risk Management Standard.
2. Process-Based Methodology: Define a process-based risk management methodology focusing on safeguarding the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information assets.
3. Documentation: Cybersecurity risk management methodology and procedures must be defined, documented, and approved.
4. Annual Review: Risk Assessment Report and Risk Management methodology must be reviewed annually.
5. Reporting: Risk Assessment results must be reported to the cybersecurity/information security committee annually.
6. Implementation: Implement the cybersecurity risk management methodology and procedures for cybersecurity assets.
7. Project Integration: Integrate cybersecurity risk management into the project management methodology.
8. Stage Identification: Identify and mitigate risks at each stage of the project.
9. Periodic Review: Review cybersecurity requirements in project management periodically.
10. Outsourcing: Conduct risk assessment for outsourcing and third-party services engagements.
11. Change Management: Include risk assessment as part of the Change Management Methodology.
