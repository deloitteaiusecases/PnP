## Purpose

The purpose of this manual is to establish standardized, traceable, and compliant procedures for all feed manufacturing activities. It serves as a core reference to:
 Define clear policies and procedures across all stages of feed production from raw material intake to finished product dispatch.
 Ensure alignment with national and international regulatory standards (e.g., SFDA, GMP+, ISO 22000, Codex Alimentarius).
 Maintain traceability and data integrity through SAP-linked workflows and documentation.
 Promote hygienic practices and process discipline to uphold feed safety and quality.
 Support cross-functional coordination to improve operational reliability and accountability.
 Serve as a training and audit resource for both new and existing staff.
 Drive continuous improvement through structured documentation and corrective action mechanisms.
This manual reflects Arabian Mills’ commitment to quality, compliance, and excellence in feed production.
