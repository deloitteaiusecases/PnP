## Introduction

This Feed Mill Policy and Procedure (P&P) Manual provides a unified operational framework for the feed mill located adjacent to the flour mill at Arabian Mills. The facility is responsible for producing a variety of animal feeds using flour milling by-products and other approved ingredients. Given the direct correlation between feed quality and animal health, this manual emphasizes standardized practices that ensure product safety, regulatory compliance, and operational consistency.
The manual incorporates SAP system integration to align physical operations with digital transactions, enabling traceability, efficient inventory management, and real-time process visibility. It also promotes a cross-functional approach, involving Quality Assurance, Production, Maintenance, Supply Chain, Procurement, and EHS, to ensure end-to-end process control and accountability.
This document is considered a living manual and will be periodically updated to reflect changes in regulations, internal audits, process improvements, and technological upgrades.
