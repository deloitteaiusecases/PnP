## Operational Security

1. Operating Procedures: Document operating procedures and make them available to all users who need them.
2. Operational Activities: Prepare documented procedures for operational activities associated with information processing and communication facilities.
3. Change Control: Control changes to Arabian Mills' business processes, information processing facilities, and systems that affect information security.
4. Risk Assessment: Conduct risk assessments before changes are initiated.
5. Management Responsibilities: Ensure formal management responsibilities and procedures are in place to control all changes satisfactorily.
6. Resource Monitoring: Monitor the use of resources, tune systems, and project future capacity requirements to ensure required system performance.
7. Capacity Requirements: Identify capacity requirements considering the business criticality of the concerned system.
8. Trend Analysis: Account for new business and system requirements and current and projected trends in Arabian Mills' information processing capabilities.
9. Environment Separation: Separate development, testing, and operational environments to reduce risks of unauthorized access or changes to the operating environment.
10. Software Installation Control: Implement procedures to control the installation of software on operational systems.
