## Information Security Reviews & Audits

1. Independent Review: Independently review the approach to managing information security at planned intervals or when significant changes occur.
2. Compliance Review: Conduct regular reviews for compliance with security policies, standards, and other requirements.
3. Audit Planning: Carefully plan and agree upon audit requirements and activities to minimize disruptions.
4. System Review: Regularly review information systems for compliance with information security policies and standards.
5. Independent Audits: Conduct cybersecurity audits and reviews independently to assess compliance with cybersecurity controls.
6. Documentation: Document and present results from cybersecurity audits and reviews to the cybersecurity/information security committee.
