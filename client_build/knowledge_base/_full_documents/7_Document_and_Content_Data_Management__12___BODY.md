## The Data Management Domains
The NDMO data management domains will enable all data stakeholders from across the  adopt a structured approach necessary to define and execute the data management initiatives within the . The data management framework is represented in “Figure 1 – KSA Data Management and Personal Data Protection Framework . The NDMO Domains comprises of 15 domains as shown below.

## Data Governance
In accordance with the business's Data Management and Personal Data Protection Strategy, data governance provides the authority and control over the development and implementation of the data management practices of the  through people, processes, and technologies.

## Data Catalog and Metadata
Data Catalog and Metadata focuses on enabling an effective access to high quality integrated metadata. The access to metadata is supported by use of the Data Catalog automated tool acting as the single point of reference to the s' metadata.

## Data Quality
Data Quality measures how fit the data is for its intended use with respect to its accuracy, completeness, integrity, timeliness, conformity and consistency.

## Data Operations
The Data Operations domain focuses on the design, implementation, and support for data storage to maximize data value throughout its lifecycle from creation/acquisition to disposal.

## Document and Content Management
Document and Content Management involves controlling the capture, storage, access, and use of documents and content stored outside of relational databases.

## Data Architecture and Modelling
Data Architecture and Modelling focuses on establishment of formal data structures and data flow channels to enable end to end data processing across and within entities.

## Reference and Master Data Management
Reference and Master Data Management allow to link all critical data to a single master file, providing a common point of reference for all critical data.

## Business Intelligence and Analytics
Business Intelligence and Analytics is concerned with the analysis of data records kept by s in order to gain insight and make judgements about the information discovered.

## Data Sharing and Interoperability
Data Sharing and Interoperability involves gathering data from diverse sources and comprises of integration strategies that promote smooth internal and external communication between various IT components. Data sharing processes that enable a structured and uniform exchange of data between entities are also covered by data sharing and interoperability.

## Data Value Realization
This process entails continuously assessing data assets for possible data-driven use cases that might increase revenue or lower operating expenses for the firm.

## Open Data
Open Data focuses on the 's data that may be made accessible to the general public to improve transparency, spur innovation, and support economic growth.

## Freedom of Information
Freedom of Information domain focuses on providing Saudi citizens access to government information, portraying the process for accessing such information, and the appeal mechanism in the event of a dispute.

## Data classification
Data Classification involves the categorization of data so that it may be used and protected efficiently. Data Classification levels are assigned following an impact assessment determining the potential damages caused by the mishandling of data or unauthorized access to data.

## Personal Data Protection
Personal Data Protection focuses on protection of a subject’s entitlement to the proper handling and non-disclosure of their personal information.

## Data Security and Protection
Data Security and Protection focuses on the processes, people, and technology designed to protect the entity’s data, including, but not limited to authorized access to data, avoidance of spoliation, and safeguarding against unauthorized disclosure of data. This domain is under the mandate of the Saudi National Cybersecurity Authority.
