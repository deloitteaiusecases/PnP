Introduction and Background of the Section

The Maintenance Department must ensure all technicians and supervisors are equipped with task-appropriate PPE, trained in its correct use, and held accountable for wearing it. PPE selection is task-based and must align with the identified risks from Task Hazard Analyses (THA) and work permit requirements.
While the Safety Department is responsible for issuing PPE guidelines and training, the Maintenance Supervisor ensures enforcement, and each technician must personally commit to its proper usage.
Policy
All maintenance personnel at Arabian Mills shall use the required Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) appropriate to the nature of the task, work environment, and identified hazards. PPE shall be worn without exception in designated areas and during tasks where risk cannot be fully controlled through engineering or administrative means.

PPE Matrix by Task Type and Hazard

| Task Type | Primary Hazards | Required PPE |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mechanical Repairs (Rotating Equipment) | Entanglement, impact, sharp edges | Safety gloves, safety shoes, eye protection, bump cap |
| Electrical Work (Live or Panel Access) | Electric shock, arc flash | Insulated gloves, arc-rated coveralls, face shield, rubber mat, safety shoes |
| Confined Space Entry | Oxygen deficiency, toxic gases, engulfment | Full body harness with retrieval, helmet with chin strap, chemical gloves, gas detector, safety boots |
| Welding / Cutting / Hot Work | Burns, sparks, fumes, eye damage | Welding helmet, fire-resistant clothing, leather gloves, apron, safety shoes |
| Working at Height | Falls, dropped tools | Harness with double lanyard, helmet with chin strap, safety shoes, hi-vis vest |
| Handling Chemicals / Lubricants | Skin/eye irritation, chemical burns | Chemical-resistant gloves, goggles, face shield, apron, respirator (if fumes) |
| Dusty Environment / Cleaning Silos | Dust inhalation, eye exposure | Dust mask or respirator, goggles, coveralls, safety shoes |
| Compressed Air / Pneumatic Systems | High-pressure release, noise | Hearing protection, safety gloves, face shield, safety shoes |
