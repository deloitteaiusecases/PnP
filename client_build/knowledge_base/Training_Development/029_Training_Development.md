# Training & Development

Introduction and Background of the Section
A skilled, competent, and safety-conscious maintenance team is critical to the operational excellence, reliability, and compliance performance of Arabian Mills. With increasing reliance on technology, automation, digital systems (such as SAP PM), and advanced diagnostics, the maintenance workforce must possess both technical depth and procedural discipline.
Training and development in the Maintenance Department must not be limited to ad hoc or reactive events. It should be a structured, competency-based program that ensures:
 Ongoing upgrading of skills aligned with changing technologies and best practices
 Safe and standardized execution of critical maintenance activities
 Readiness of personnel to handle both routine and emergency tasks
 Cross-functional capability to reduce reliance on individuals
 Compliance with health, safety, and food industry regulatory expectations
This section defines the policies and procedures for managing the continuous professional development of maintenance staff.
Policy
Arabian Mills shall maintain a structured training and development program for all maintenance personnel, aligned with their job roles and the technical, safety, and compliance requirements of the plant. Each maintenance employee shall have a defined training matrix and skill development plan.
Training shall cover:
 Equipment-specific maintenance practices
 Preventive and corrective maintenance techniques
 SAP PM usage (notifications, work orders, confirmations, reporting)
 Calibration and testing
 Spare parts and inventory processes
 Permit-to-work system and safety compliance (THA, PPE, LOTO)
 Soft skills including communication, problem-solving, and teamwork
Training needs shall be assessed annually and upon job role changes, and all training activities shall be documented, tracked, and reviewed for effectiveness.
