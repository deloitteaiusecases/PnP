# Performance Monitoring

Introduction and Background of the Section
Performance monitoring is the foundation of a data-driven maintenance strategy. By systematically tracking key performance indicators (KPIs), Arabian Mills can evaluate the efficiency, effectiveness, and reliability of its maintenance operations. These insights enable proactive decision-making, support resource optimization, and enhance overall plant uptime and safety.
This section aims to establish a complete and standardized performance monitoring framework aligned with global best practices, integrated into SAP PM, and reviewed at appropriate management levels.
Policy
Arabian Mills shall follow a structured maintenance performance monitoring system that:
 Tracks operational, reliability, and compliance-related KPIs in line with global benchmarks (e.g., SMRP, ISO 55000)
 Uses SAP PM as the official data source for recording and reporting KPIs
 Reviews KPIs at regular intervals to support preventive and corrective actions
 Aligns KPI trends with continuous improvement initiatives and maintenance budgeting
 Assigns ownership of data accuracy, reporting, and corrective actions to defined roles
KPIs shall be visible to both the maintenance and production departments and reviewed jointly to ensure alignment on plant availability and performance.
