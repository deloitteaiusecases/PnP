## Reporting and Analysis

Introduction and Background of the Section
A well-structured reporting and analysis framework transforms raw maintenance data into actionable insights. At Arabian Mills, where the maintenance team is increasingly adopting SAP PM and formalized KPIs, it is essential to ensure that the right data is reported at the right level and analyzed consistently for operational and strategic improvement.
The purpose is to establish how KPI data is extracted, analyzed, communicated, and acted upon—turning raw metrics into operational and strategic decisions
Procedure

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Extract KPI Data from SAP**<br>• Pull data using reports, queries, or dashboards aligned with defined KPI metrics in 1.11.1. | Planner / SAP PM Administrator | Weekly / Monthly |
| 2 | **Prepare and Format Visual Reports**<br>• Structure the data by category (e.g., reliability, cost) and format it for review meetings. | Maintenance Manager | Monthly |
| 3 | **Analyze KPI Trends**<br>• Compare actual performance against targets, baselines, or prior periods. Highlight abnormal trends. | Maintenance Manager | Monthly |
| 4 | **Conduct Monthly KPI Review Meeting**<br>• Discuss trends, root causes, and potential solutions with relevant maintenance staff. | Maintenance Director | Monthly |
| 5 | **Escalate Deviations for RCA**<br>• Trigger structured root cause analysis for underperforming KPIs (e.g., repeated failures, high MTTR). | Maintenance Manager | As Needed |
| 6 | **Assign and Track Corrective Actions**<br>• Record ownership, timeline, and status of agreed action items from reviews. | Maintenance Supervisor | Monthly / Ongoing |
| 7 | **Submit Management Report**<br>• Present high-level KPI summary to senior management with analysis and improvement plans. | Maintenance Director | Monthly / Quarterly |
| 8 | **Link Trends to Strategic Planning**<br>• Use KPI analysis to revise PM frequencies, training needs, manpower plans, and budget inputs. | Maintenance Director / Finance Team | Annually |

KPI and Report Relevance by Role

| Role | Relevant KPIs / Reports | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Technicians | **- Work Order Completion Rate**<br>• - Wrench Time (%)<br>• - Feedback Accuracy<br>• - THA & LOTO Compliance | To reinforce individual accountability for work execution, documentation, and safety compliance |
| Maintenance Supervisors | **- Schedule Compliance**<br>• - Emergency Work Ratio<br>• - LOTO & THA Compliance<br>• - Permit Issuance Accuracy<br>• - Safety Incidents | To monitor day-to-day execution, adherence to procedure, and front-line safety performance |
| Maintenance Manager | **- MTBF**<br>• - MTTR<br>• - Repeat Failures<br>• - Energy Consumption per Ton<br>• - Calibration Compliance Rate<br>• - Backlog Age<br>• - Spare Parts vs. Budget | To identify technical and systemic problems, analyze asset health, and plan reliability improvements |
| Maintenance Director | **- Planned Maintenance Compliance (PMC)**<br>• - Maintenance Cost per Ton<br>• - KPI Trends Dashboard<br>• - RCA Reports - Budget vs. Actual | To ensure strategic performance, policy adherence, cost control, and department alignment |
| Senior Management | **- Downtime Hours**<br>• - Maintenance Cost per Ton<br>• - Emergency Work Ratio<br>• - High-level KPI Summary | To assess how maintenance supports production goals, controls cost, and manages operational risk |

Notes:
 Technicians and supervisors use operational KPIs tied to job performance and compliance.
 Managers and planners focus on analytical and reliability KPIs to improve systems.
 Directors and senior leaders need aggregated reports aligned with cost, downtime, and strategic targets.
Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Technician | • - Cooperate in RCA reviews if technician input or behavior contributed to KPI deviation.<br>• - Learn from review outcomes. |
| Maintenance Supervisor | • - Participate in monthly KPI reviews.<br>• - Track assigned actions and ensure local compliance improvements are implemented. |
| Planner | • - Extract SAP KPI data for reports.<br>• - Maintain tracker of action items resulting from reviews. |
| SAP PM Administrator | - Troubleshoot any reporting gaps or misalignments in SAP dashboards or report generation. |
| Maintenance Manager | • - Analyze KPI trends and lead RCAs.<br>• - Develop insight-based corrective actions. |
| Maintenance Director | • - Lead review meetings and report results to senior management.<br>• - Approve and monitor implementation of strategic actions. |
| Finance Team | - Participate in annual review cycles to align maintenance KPIs with cost reporting and budget performance. |
| Senior Management | - Review high-level KPI summaries and use results in broader business planning and performance evaluation. |
