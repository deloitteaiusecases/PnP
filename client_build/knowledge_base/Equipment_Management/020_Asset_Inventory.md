## Asset Inventory

Introduction and Background of the Section
The asset inventory in SAP PM is the foundational dataset that enables the Maintenance Department to plan, execute, and analyse maintenance work. It includes structured information for each maintainable item in the plant, such as equipment ID, functional location, criticality, historical work orders, and associated preventive maintenance strategies.
For the maintenance function, the asset inventory is not about data entry or record creation—it is about using that data to ensure:
 Accurate job planning and resource allocation
 Equipment-specific work history review
 Preventive maintenance linkage
 Spare parts identification
 Strategic decision-making around reliability and replacement
Procedure for Asset Inventory (Usage-Focused)

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Use Equipment ID to Plan and Schedule Jobs**<br>• All work orders must reference a valid SAP equipment ID to ensure traceability. | Maintenance Planner | Per Work Order |
| 2 | **Refer to Historical Work Orders**<br>• Use equipment history in SAP to identify recurring problems, prior repairs, and past interventions. | Maintenance Manager | During Departmental Review |
| 3 | **Use Equipment Criticality for Prioritization**<br>• Reference asset criticality when determining scheduling and resource allocation. | Maintenance Planner | Monthly Planning |
| 4 | **Link PM Task Lists and Strategies**<br>• Ensure all preventive maintenance activities are tied to specific equipment IDs and follow correct intervals. | Maintenance Planner | Per PM Plan Update |
| 5 | **Identify Spare Parts via Equipment BOM**<br>• View spare parts list for each asset directly from the SAP equipment screen during planning or job execution. | Maintenance Planner | Per Job |
| 6 | **Review Cost and Downtime History**<br>• Evaluate long-term cost trends and downtime data for assets to support replacement or upgrade justification. | Maintenance Manager | Quarterly |
| 7 | **Flag Data Inconsistencies or Gaps**<br>• Report missing, inaccurate, or duplicated equipment records to the SAP administrator for correction. | Maintenance Planner | As Needed |

Roles and Responsibilities – Asset Inventory (Maintenance Use Only)

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Technician | Use equipment BOMs in SAP to request parts and reference work order history where available |
| Maintenance Planner | Schedule jobs using equipment IDs, prioritize based on criticality, and verify correct spare part linkages |
| Maintenance Manager | Review historical data to support analysis and improvement, identify gaps or anomalies in records |
| **Maintenance Supervisor**<br>• + B,C, shift supervisor | Use equipment priority and history for short-term planning and escalation decisions |
| Maintenance Director | Use asset-level data for strategic decisions on replacements, upgrades, and resource planning |
