## Equipment Criticality Assessment

Introduction and Background of the Section
Equipment criticality assessment is a core element of risk-based maintenance planning. It enables the Maintenance Department to allocate time, resources, and preventive focus according to the relative importance and risk associated with each asset.
In a facility like Arabian Mills—where continuous operations, food safety, and machine reliability are essential—the criticality of each asset must reflect its impact on safety, production, quality, cost, and compliance. Criticality ratings guide:
 Preventive maintenance prioritization
 Spare parts stocking decisions
 Response times and work order prioritization
 Inspection and monitoring frequencies
 Capital replacement planning
Criticality classifications must be documented in SAP PM and periodically reviewed to reflect changes in plant layout, process flow, or equipment function.
Procedure for Equipment Criticality Assessment

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Define Criticality Criteria**<br>• Use a multi-factor model based on safety risk, production impact, quality effect, cost of failure, and redundancy. | Maintenance Manager | Once / As Updated |
| 2 | **Assess Equipment Criticality**<br>• Evaluate each asset based on defined criteria and assign a rating (e.g., Critical, Semi-Critical, Non-Critical). | Maintenance Manager | Initially |
| 3 | **Review with Stakeholders**<br>• Review proposed ratings with Production, Quality, and Safety teams to ensure cross-functional alignment. | Maintenance Director | Annually / As Needed |
| 4 | **Enter Criticality in SAP**<br>• Update the SAP equipment master with the approved criticality classification for each asset. | SAP PM Administrator + | As Updated |
| 5 | **Use Criticality in Planning**<br>• Reference criticality in preventive maintenance planning, work order prioritization, and resource allocation. | Maintenance Planner | Ongoing |
| 6 | **Review and Reassess**<br>• Reassess equipment criticality if there is a process change, repeated failure, or equipment relocation. | Maintenance Manager | As Needed |

Asset Criticality Assessment Criteria
(Risk-Based Model for Maintenance Prioritization with Assigned Responsibility)

| Assessment Factor | Scoring Range | Description | Rated By |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Safety Impact | 1–5 | Likelihood of harm to personnel or risk to plant safety if asset fails | Safety Officer |
| 2. Production Impact | 1–5 | Degree to which a failure causes downtime or output loss | Production Manager |
| 3. Quality or Regulatory Impact | 1–5 | Potential to cause non-conformance to food/feed quality or regulatory standards | QA Officer |
| 4. Cost of Downtime or Repair | 1–5 | Financial consequence of failure including repair cost and lost production | Maintenance Manager |
| 5. Redundancy or Backup | 1–5 | Availability of backup systems or workarounds (lower score = more backup exists) | Maintenance Planner |

Total Score = Sum of all 5 factors (Range: 5 to 25)
Final rating to be reviewed and approved by the Maintenance Director.
Criticality Classification Table

| Total Score | Classification | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 21–25 | Critical | High risk to safety, production, and compliance. No backup. Failure must be prevented. |
| 15–20 | Semi-Critical | Moderate impact. Limited redundancy. Requires regular monitoring and scheduled preventive maintenance. |
| 5–14 | Non-Critical | Low consequence. Redundant or non-essential asset. Maintenance can be low-frequency or run-to-failure. |

Roles and Responsibilities – Equipment Criticality Assessment

| Role | Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Maintenance Manager | Define criteria and assess asset criticality |
| Maintenance Planner | Use criticality in job scheduling, preventive planning, and prioritization |
| SAP PM Administrator | Ensure criticality data is correctly entered and maintained in SAP |
| Maintenance Manager | Approve criticality methodology and updates; use criticality data for planning and performance monitoring |
| Production / Safety / QA | Participate in cross-functional review to validate criticality assessments where relevant |
| Maintenance Director | Lead criticality rating reviews with cross-functional teams |
