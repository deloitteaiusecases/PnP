## Equipment Calibration and Testing

Introduction and Background of the Section
Calibration and functional testing of instruments, sensors, and control devices are essential for ensuring the accuracy, reliability, and safety of operations in food and feed manufacturing. Measurements such as temperature, pressure, flow, weight, and speed affect not only process control and quality, but also legal compliance and auditability.
At Arabian Mills, the Maintenance Department is responsible for ensuring that all calibration-required equipment is identified, scheduled for calibration, tested against standard references, and updated in the SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) system. Testing and calibration must follow defined protocols, use traceable standards, and be performed by qualified internal personnel or approved external service providers.
Procedure for Calibration and Testing

| S. No. | Procedure Description | Responsibility | Frequency |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Identify Calibration-Critical Equipment**<br>• List all instruments whose accuracy affects product quality, compliance, or safety. | Maintenance Manager | As Needed |
| 2 | **Define Calibration Frequencies**<br>• Assign calibration/test intervals based on manufacturer guidance, usage conditions, and risk level. | Maintenance Manager | Per Asset |
| 3 | **Plan Calibration Tasks in SAP**<br>• Include calibration activities in preventive maintenance schedules using SAP task lists and strategies. | Maintenance Planner | Per Calibration Cycle |
| 4 | **Execute Calibration or Testing**<br>• Perform activity using traceable reference equipment, following standard test methods. | External Contractor | Per Schedule |
| 5 | **Record Calibration Results in SAP**<br>• Attach calibration certificates or test reports to the relevant work order. | SAP PM Administrator | Per Calibration |
| 6 | **Assess Equipment Found Out of Tolerance**<br>• Review impact of deviation on product/process and initiate corrective actions if required. | Maintenance Engineer / QA | As Needed |
| 7 | **Maintain Calibration Records**<br>• Ensure all historical calibration data is retrievable in SAP for audit and analysis. | SAP PM Administrator | Ongoing |
| 8 | **Review Calibration Effectiveness**<br>• Analyse trends in deviations, repeat failures, or contractor non-compliance to improve the program. | Maintenance Director | Quarterly |

Calibration Equipment Scope
Typical calibration/testing targets in wheat milling and feed production include:
 Temperature sensors (e.g., duct, oil bath, cooling systems)
 Weighing systems (bagging lines, batching systems)
 Flow meters (liquid dosing, pneumatic transport)
 Pressure gauges and transmitters
 Speed sensors and tachometers
 Proximity and limit switches affecting safety interlocks

Roles and Responsibilities – Calibration and Testing

| Role | Strategic Responsibilities |
| --- | --- |
| Maintenance Manager | Identify calibration scope, define frequencies, assess tolerance deviations, and approve technical procedures |
| Maintenance Planner | Schedule calibration tasks in SAP and coordinate timing with production |
| External Contractor | Perform accredited calibrations where required; submit certificates and compliance records |
| SAP PM Administrator | Link calibration tasks to equipment in SAP, attach test documents, and ensure traceability |
| Maintenance Director | Oversee calibration program effectiveness, contractor performance, and overall compliance |
| QA / Compliance Officer | Review impact of out-of-tolerance instruments and validate resolution of related quality or audit risks |
