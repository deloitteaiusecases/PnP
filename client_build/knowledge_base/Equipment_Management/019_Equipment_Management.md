# Equipment Management

Introduction and Background of the Section
Effective equipment management is central to maintaining the performance, reliability, and safety of the plant. In the maintenance context, this refers not to the physical handling or procurement of equipment, but to the creation, control, and continuous improvement of equipment-related technical records and maintenance master data in the SAP system.
A well-maintained and complete equipment inventory in SAP allows the Maintenance Department to:
 Plan and schedule preventive maintenance
 Track equipment-specific work history and costs
 Identify trends in breakdowns and repair patterns
 Link spare parts, task lists, and safety protocols to assets
 Support lifecycle decisions and plant optimization
Policy
The Maintenance Department at Arabian Mills shall utilize the SAP Plant Maintenance (PM) system to manage and interact with equipment records in support of effective maintenance planning, execution, and continuous improvement. The equipment inventory shall serve as the official reference for all maintainable assets, including their technical specifications, functional locations, criticality classifications, and maintenance history.
The Maintenance Department is responsible for:
 Utilizing the asset inventory to schedule and prioritize maintenance work
 Accessing and interpreting equipment history and cost trends for reliability analysis
 Referencing spare parts BOMs and technical details during job planning and execution
 Ensuring that preventive maintenance strategies are aligned with equipment characteristics and operational demands
In addition, the department shall:
 Maintain and periodically review equipment criticality classifications to guide risk-based maintenance decisions, optimize resource allocation, and support production continuity
 Ensure all calibration-eligible equipment is identified, scheduled for calibration, and tested in accordance with defined standards and regulatory requirements, with full traceability in SAP PM
All updates to equipment data, including changes in location, criticality, or calibration requirements, shall be promptly reported and reflected in the SAP system to maintain data integrity.
