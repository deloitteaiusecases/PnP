# APPENDIX A		Interaction with the Projects & Automation Department

Automation systems play a vital role in the efficient operation, reliability, and continuous improvement of the plant. At Arabian Mills, the Automation Team operates under the Projects & Automation Department, which has a separate reporting line to its own Director. However, due to the highly technical and integrated nature of automated control systems with plant equipment, close coordination with the Engineering & Maintenance is essential.
A large part of the Automation Team’s workload supports technical maintenance, reliability, and performance improvement activities. This makes their integration into the maintenance planning and execution framework a strategic necessity.
Scope of Collaboration
The collaboration between the Automation and Maintenance departments encompasses the following key areas:
1. Preventive and Corrective Maintenance
  o Automation engineers participate in scheduled and unscheduled maintenance activities, especially where PLCs, sensors, and control systems are involved.
2. Direct Coordination with Electrical Maintenance
  o Most control systems interface with power, drive, and motor systems; therefore, coordination with the electrical team is essential to ensure system integrity.
3. Root Cause Analysis (RCA)
  o The Automation Team contributes control system data, diagnostics, and logic reviews to support effective RCA and implementation of sustainable solutions.
4. Energy Efficiency
  o Automation systems monitor critical energy parameters and support optimization through data analysis and control logic tuning.
5. Process Sequencing and Safety Interlocks
  o The team ensures operational sequencing aligns with safety, quality, and production requirements, especially during equipment start-ups or changeovers.
6. PLC and HMI System Maintenance
  o Regular maintenance of software and hardware components, troubleshooting, logic modifications, and firmware updates are carried out jointly or in coordination with maintenance teams.
Capex and Project Integration
The Projects & Automation Department is also responsible for:
 Identifying and budgeting annual BMR (Balancing, Modernization, and Replacement) projects
 Conducting the technical evaluation of capex projects jointly with the Engineering & Maintenance department
 Overseeing project installation and commissioning, which is executed by the Engineering team

This collaboration is reflected across several procedures in the manual:
